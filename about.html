About the World Chess Championship 2023

The World Chess Championship is the most prestigious event in the chess world, where the top chess players from around the globe compete for the title of World Chess Champion.

Key features of this championship:

  • Happend between GM Ding Liren and GM Ian Nepomniachtchi
  • GM Magnus Carlsen did not defend his World Champion Title
  • Hosted in Astana, Kazakhstan
  • Followed Best of 14 Games format with Tie-Breaks if Required
  • Intense battles showcasing exceptional chess strategies
  • Historic event with a rich tradition
